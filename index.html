ACRONAME

How to Play

  1. 1 Both players get the same random acronym (e.g. H.A.M.)
  2. 2 Each player picks a category for the OTHER player (e.g. "Band Name," "Secret Society")
  3. 3 Make up a name that fits your acronym AND category!
  4. 4 Reveal your creations and laugh!

Remote play: Share links back and forth. No sign-up needed!
